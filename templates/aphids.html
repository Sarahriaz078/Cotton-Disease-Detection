Aphids Disease Information

Overview

Aphids are small sap-sucking insects that can cause significant damage to cotton plants. They feed on the plant's juices, weakening the plant and transmitting harmful viruses. Infestations can lead to reduced yield and poor fiber quality.

Symptoms

  • Yellowing or curling of leaves
  • Sticky substance (honeydew) on leaves and stems
  • Presence of ants around the plant
  • Distorted or stunted growth

Control & Cure

  • Introduce natural predators like ladybugs or lacewings
  • Use neem oil or insecticidal soaps
  • Apply recommended insecticides if infestation is severe
  • Practice crop rotation and proper field sanitation
